How To Play

Follow the steps below to play Stellar Dice:

  1. To Start, Click on 'Start Playing' button
  2. Once in the gameboard, Click on 'Roll Dice' button to begin the match
  3. The game is played with a pair of single six-sided dice
  4. You can roll your pair of dice up to 3 times
  5. After 3 rounds, the game is over and the final result is shown on the screen
  6. If you want to play more, click on the 'New Game' button
  7. You can play as many rounds as you wish! Good luck!
