Rules

Stellar Dice consists of 3 total rounds.

Each round, the player and the computer will roll a pair of dice to establish a round score.

The player with the highest score after 3 rounds is declared the winner.

If either player rolls a 1 on either dice, round score is 0.

If either player rolls matching dice values, round score is double the total of both dice.

For any other dice combination of numbers, the round score is the total of two dice.
